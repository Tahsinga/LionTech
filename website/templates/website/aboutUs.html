Our Story

Founded in Harare, Lion Tech Zimbabwe has grown from a small tech shop to a leading technology provider in the region.

We started with a simple mission: to make quality technology accessible to everyone in Zimbabwe. Today, we're proud to offer a wide range of products and services that help our customers stay connected and productive in an increasingly digital world.

Our team of tech enthusiasts is always on the lookout for the latest innovations to bring to our customers. We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust, quality, and excellent service.

Our Journey

2015

Founded as a small computer repair shop in central Harare with just 2 employees.

2017

Opened our first retail location on Jason Moyo Avenue, expanding our product offerings.

2019

Grew to a team of 10 technicians and sales associates, serving over 1,000 customers monthly.

2021

Recognized as "Best Tech Retailer" in Harare by the Zimbabwe Tech Association.

2023

Expanded to our current location at Stanley House, doubling our showroom space.
